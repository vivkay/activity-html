Why Oliver Jeffers is my Hero…

Oliver Jeffers is an artist and children's book author from Ireland. Some of his classic kids books include:

Lost and Found

How to Catch a Star

The Incredible Book Eating Boy

and

That Moose Belongs to Me

… now these are just a handful of over fifteen adorable children's books that Oliver Jeffers has written and/or illustrated!

The Coolest Thing About Oliver Jeffers

Oliver Jeffers' publisher, Harper Collins Children's Books, had his book The Heart and the Bottle adapted into an interactive storybook on the ipad, narrated by UK actress Helena Bonham Carter.

In this interactive app, users use the touch-screen to interact with Jeffers' artwork, including

  1. lighting stars in a night sky
  2. making ripples in water, and
  3. creating their own painting>
